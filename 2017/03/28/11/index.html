Exhibition

2017-03-28

In week 10 of this semester, our programme organized a public show for coursework at informatics forum. It was a great opportunity for us to test our installation and find some space for improvement. Apart from video part, we need to test physical stuff such as wheel speed sensor, brake buttons and bicycle stander. To ensure our project will work successfully, we did several rounds of testing for Arduino, Processing and projectors, and we integrated separate parts together for exhibition.

In order to provide immersive experience, we chose to create three screens for display content. The main screen in front of space is for video, but two sides of screen is for pop up of images. As projectors and cables are delicate, we use yellow tape to guide users to get in the bicycle instead of intrude into the mess space. We got highly response during the exhibition, and attracted different types of users.

We had approximately 50 visitors during the exhibition. After the exhibition, we get many valuable feedbacks from the visitor and also from the teacher. In general, the visitors enjoy the experience.They have positive feedbacks about the physical experience and the function that they can stop and have a look about the story of the location. As for suggestion, there are two problems mentioned by most people and also by teacher. The first one is they feel the interaction style is linear after the excitement point of the experienceï¼Œ the other one is that sometimes, they can not stop in the exact location that they want.

Therefore, we made some improvements. First of all, we divided whole video into three video clips in order to offer flexible route choices, which is Princes Street area, Royal Mile area and Holyrood Park area. In this situation, users will not get bored due to fixed route, instead, they will accomplish the journey easily and relaxing. Next is modification of instruction page. Actually there is an image at beginning of system that instruct users how to use the bicycle. Unfortunately, most of people ignored that page and forgot how to use system. Therefore, we decided to add an instruction text to remind users at every memory point. Last, we use only one projector screen instead of three screens due to losing coherence. According to feedback, users could not build connection between video and images that pop up in the two side screens. Therefore, we put images with memories descriptions to be shown in the main screen together for better understanding of project.
